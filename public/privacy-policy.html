Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 26, 2025

IAS Solution places great importance on protecting your personal data. This privacy policy informs you about how we collect, use and protect your information in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable French legislation.

1. Data Controller

Company: IAS Solution

Contact: support@ia-solution.fr

Website: https://ia-solution.fr

IAS Solution is the data controller for your personal data collected through our website and mobile applications (including Equi Saddles).

2. Data Collected and Purposes

2.1 Data Collected via Mobile Applications

Our mobile applications (Equi Saddles and others) are designed to respect your privacy. By default, no personal data is automatically collected by the application.

Important: IAS Solution applications operate autonomously and do not transmit any personal information to our servers without your explicit consent.

2.2 Data Collected via the Website

When you visit our website ia-solution.fr, we may collect the following information:

  • Browsing data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, visit duration
  • Contact data: If you contact us (name, email, message)
  • Technical cookies: Necessary for proper website functioning

2.3 Processing Purposes

Your data is collected for the following purposes:

  • Improve user experience on our site
  • Respond to your contact requests
  • Ensure security and stability of our services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Conduct anonymized visitor statistics

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Personal data processing by IAS Solution is based on:

  • Your consent: For non-essential cookies and communications
  • Legitimate interest: For site security and service improvement
  • Contract execution: For providing our services
  • Legal obligations: For data retention required by law

4. Your Rights (GDPR)

In accordance with GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: Obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Right of rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to restriction: Limit processing of your data
  • Right to object: Object to processing of your data
  • Right to portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to withdraw consent: At any time
How to exercise your rights?
To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: support@ia-solution.fr
We will respond within one month.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies. For more information about our use of cookies, please consult our Cookie Policy.

6. Contact

For any questions concerning this privacy policy or exercising your rights, you can contact us at:

Email: support@ia-solution.fr

Website: https://ia-solution.fr
